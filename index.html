Hello! My name is Sekhmet, I'm a 4.5 years old tabby cat. My mommy named me after an Egyptian goddess although I'm a boy, haha!
I love cuddles and I especially love when they rub my belly. I start purring so much if you do that! I also love giving little head or nose bumps to show my affection. I'm very shy with people I don't know, so if a stranger comes home you'll definitely see me running to hide under the bed! But after a while I'll gain my courage and become confident enough to get out and try to get to know them. So all in all I'm not so different from the majority of cats... But my mommy believes that I'm the cutest cat ever and she covers me with kisses and cuddles!

What I like to do

My most favourite thing ever is to sit on my former scratching tree that mommy put on the balcony. It's the perfect place to have a view on the world, and especially to look at the birds... I love it so much that no matter the weather, even with the rain or the snow I have to go there!

When the weather is nice, mommy puts a harness and a leash on me and she brings me to some big park with lots of grass and trees. If I feel confident enough, I like exploring the area and munching the grass... but since I'm shy with other people around, it really needs to be an isolated area for me to do it!

I'm a cat, so obviously my most favourite thing ever is sleeping. Mommy attached a cat hammock on the heater, so during the winter, when the heating is on, that area becomes so warm and comfy. I could sleep there the whole day, and sometimes you'll find me lying in there in the most funny positions, like in this picture!

Catwalk

I'm also... a model?! Well, every now and then my mommy gets me some fancy clothing to take cute pictures of me. Some examples are a kimono-like shirt she got in Japan, a very cool bandana with my name that she got from Mr.Bandana, and a pair of sunglasses with a hoodie! :3

My favourite food

I absolutely love chicken! So of course I'll totally eat whatever chicken-based food you'll give me. But I also like fish, so my mommy can manage to keep my diet varied between different flavours. My favourite food brand ever is Catz Fine Food. Whenever she opens one of those cans, no matter if I'm sleeping or not: I'll immediately recognize the sound and run there to enjoy that delicious food!
